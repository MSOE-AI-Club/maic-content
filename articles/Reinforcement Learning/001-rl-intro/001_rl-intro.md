## What is Reinforcement Learning?

Reinforcement Learning (RL) is a branch of machine learning where an agent learns by interacting with its environment to achieve goals. Unlike supervised learning, where models learn from labeled data, RL agents learn from trial and error, receiving feedback in the form of rewards or penalties based on their actions.
